# Mock Discovery Session

## Organization Profile
- Company: Northstar Health Operations
- Department: Revenue Cycle Operations
- Team size: 42
- Region: US

## Current State
- AI tool access exists, but usage is inconsistent.
- Most team members still use manual drafting for claims follow-up notes.
- Managers are supportive but have no structured onboarding path.

## Adoption Blockers (Ranked)
1. Low confidence in output quality
2. Unclear policy boundaries for sensitive data
3. No repeatable workflow for daily use

## Constraints
- No patient-identifiable data can be entered into AI tools.
- Team can allocate 90 minutes per week for onboarding.
- Pilot must show measurable benefit within 4 weeks.

## Candidate Pilot Workflow
- Claims follow-up note drafting and quality review

## Success Criteria
- 20% reduction in draft turnaround time
- 15% reduction in revision loops
- Increased self-reported confidence from participants

## Open Questions
- Which manager will own week-by-week review cadence?
- Which quality baseline source is most reliable today?
