# Output Quality Rubric

Use this rubric to evaluate generated 4-week onboarding plans before sharing.

## Scoring

Score each category from 1 (weak) to 5 (strong):

1. Relevance
- Does the program reflect the specific discovery context?

2. Feasibility
- Can the team execute this with available time and constraints?

3. Actionability
- Are weekly exercises concrete and runnable?

4. Confidence Building
- Does the plan progressively reduce hesitation and build trust?

5. Workflow Realism
- Is the manual-to-AI conversion credible for this team?

6. Measurement Clarity
- Are success signals explicit and trackable by week 4?

## Total Score
- /30

## Interpretation
- 25-30: strong candidate for pilot
- 19-24: usable with minor refinement
- 13-18: revise mapping and weekly exercises
- 6-12: restart from discovery clarity
